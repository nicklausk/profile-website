Increasing diversity and inclusion in academia is important to being able to ensure our research benefits all those in our society. Being more inclusive is everyone's responsibility, and we can always learn new ways to broaden our inclusive practices. Some ways that I have found to learn more about inclusive practices and improving myself are attending ally training, attending seminars about bystander interventions and inclusive teaching, and trying to incorporate some of these lessons in my research and teaching. I've included some resources for others to explore more about this topic, and let me know if you have other resources for me to check out and add.
